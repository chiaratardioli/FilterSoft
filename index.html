Welcome to Conjunction Assessment Page.

This is a piece of software to be integrated with the OrbFit software. It reads a catalogue of objects and systematically excludes pairs that are not at risk of conjunction. It has been tested with a fictitious population of space debris in Low-Earth orbit and with real data of objects from the NORAD website.

Procedures

After compiling the source code, go on the test folder and try:

$ ./chain_order.sh 0
$ ./filter_sequence.x

If you want to try the TLE catalogue, try instead:

$ ./cubesats_ephemerides.sh
$ ./filter_sequence.x

You can also have a look at one evolution and its interpolation. Just type:

$ ./Utest_table.x
